Plain-English summary

  • We collect information from you to administer legal aid grants on behalf of your clients.
  • We share information with LAQ grants officers, your firm, and with courts where required.
  • You can ask to see or correct any personal information we hold about you.
  • We keep records for seven years after a file is closed, then securely destroy them.
  • You must acknowledge this notice to use the Practitioner Portal.

Full notice

1. Who collects this information

Legal Aid Queensland (LAQ) is the collecting agency. LAQ is an independent statutory body established under the Legal Aid Queensland Act 1997.

2. What we collect

We collect information about you in your capacity as a legal practitioner, including:

  • Your name, firm, position, contact details, ABN and bank account details for payment of grants.
  • Your professional registration and admission details.
  • Information about grants of aid you manage, including matter type, scope, invoices, outcomes and correspondence.
  • Records of your access to the portal, for security and compliance purposes.

3. Why we collect it

We collect this information to administer the legal aid scheme under the Legal Aid Queensland Act 1997 and associated guidelines, including assessing applications, managing grants, paying practitioners and monitoring outcomes.

4. Who we share it with

  • LAQ grants officers and other LAQ staff for the administration of grants.
  • Your firm's authorised administrators, where delegated access has been configured.
  • Courts, tribunals and other agencies where required by law or with your consent.
  • Auditors and the Queensland Audit Office for compliance purposes.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, personal information that LAQ holds about you. Contact LAQ's Privacy Officer at privacy@legalaid.qld.gov.au.

6. Retention

Records are retained for seven years after a file is closed, in line with Queensland State Archives' General Retention and Disposal Schedule. After that period, records are securely destroyed.

7. Security

Information held in the Practitioner Portal is protected by Salesforce-platform security controls, including access controls, encryption in transit and at rest, and audit logging. Access is reviewed quarterly.

8. Acknowledgement

By selecting "I acknowledge" you confirm that you have read and understood this notice.
