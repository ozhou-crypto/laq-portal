Solicitor clause wording

Represent your client in relation to an application for special leave to appeal to the High Court. This is a maximum fee grant of aid (including agent's fees). Claims can be made up to the maximum fees. Proforma accounts for solicitor and barrister are enclosed. Reasonable outlays will be paid. Receipts should be retained on file. The maximum fee payable on this invoice for disbursements is $500.00 (A1).

Client clause wording

Be represented in an application for special leave to appeal to the High Court.
