# S02 — Sign-in (SSO) — In-House Solicitor

**Personas:** In-House Solicitor
**User stories:** FR-B1-302-A

## Purpose

LAQ-internal users bypass the MFA form and sign in with their LAQ identity.

## Layout

Same chrome as S01 but a single 'Sign in with LAQ' button. No username/password fields.

## Elements to include

- Branded panel identical to S01 for consistency
- Prominent 'Sign in with LAQ' brand button
- Explanatory caption: what this sign-in method is for and who should use it
- Link back to external sign-in for users who landed here in error

## States

Default, SSO redirect, access-denied (not provisioned).

## Designer notes

Visually identical shell to S01 so brand consistency is maintained across both sign-in routes.
