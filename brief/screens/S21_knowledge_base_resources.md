# S21 — Knowledge Base & resources

**Personas:** All practitioners
**User stories:** FR-B1-310, FR-B1-310-A

## Purpose

Searchable library of grants policies, handbooks, case law precedents, CPD events, and LAQ articles.

## Layout

Editorial landing with featured content at top, categories below, and a prominent search.

## Elements to include

- Hero search with type-ahead
- Featured: latest handbook updates, upcoming CPD events, recent LAQ articles
- Category tiles: Policies, Handbooks, Case law, CPD events, Articles
- Article reader view with related articles and 'was this helpful?' feedback
- Bookmarking and recently viewed

## States

Browse, search results, article reader, empty bookmarks.
